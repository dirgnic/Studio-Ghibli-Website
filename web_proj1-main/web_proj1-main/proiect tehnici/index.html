About Studio Ghibli

Studio Ghibli is a Japanese animation film studio headquartered in Tokyo, Japan.

Founded in 1985 by Hayao Miyazaki and Isao Takahata, the studio has produced some of the most acclaimed animated films in the world.

Popular Movies

Spirited Away

A young girl, Chihiro, finds herself trapped in a mysterious world of spirits and must navigate through it to save her parents and return to the human world.

Grave of the Fireflies

In the midst of World War II, a young boy and his little sister struggle to survive in war-torn Japan after their home is destroyed by firebombing.

Kiki's Delivery Service

Follow Kiki, a young witch, as she embarks on her mandatory year of independence and sets up a flying courier service in a quaint seaside town.

My Neighbor Totoro

Two young sisters, Satsuki and Mei, befriend friendly forest spirits, including the iconic Totoro, while their mother is in the hospital, bringing magic and wonder into their lives.

Popular Characters

Chihiro

Chihiro is the main protagonist of "Spirited Away." She starts off as a whiny and petulant girl but grows into a courageous and determined young woman as she navigates the spirit world to save her parents.

Seita

Seita is one of the main characters in "Grave of the Fireflies." He is a teenage boy who, along with his younger sister Setsuko, struggles to survive in war-torn Japan during World War II.

Kiki

Kiki is the titular character of "Kiki's Delivery Service." She is a young witch who sets out on a journey of self-discovery during her mandatory year of independence, learning about friendship, responsibility, and the value of hard work.

Totoro

Totoro is a gentle forest spirit and the titular character of "My Neighbor Totoro." He befriends two young sisters, Satsuki and Mei, and introduces them to the magical world of the forest, providing comfort and guidance when they need it most.
